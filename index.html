Our solution - 3 component ecosystem for disaster victims preparedness, safety, and recovery

1. SparrowNet - Network abstraction spanning various hardware and software stacks to connect disaster victims with communty near them and accross the world
2. SparrowAI - AI to aid victims and medical experts capture medical data, gain insights and make informed decisions
3. Sparrow applications ecosystem - Applets connected to SparrowNet and SparrowAI to help disaster victims, responders, medical teams and the global disater response community createred to the 'Offline first' requirements
